Service Terms

Save yourself time and hassle by booking a service call with me. I will come to you and service your bike saving you two trips into the shop and weeks of waiting in between.

Serviceable Area

My home territory is Durham Region between Pickering and Bowmanville. Travel fees may apply and are based on $0.50/kilometer. This fee is per visit and not per bike. Travel fees are never included with service prices.

On-Site

I perform all service on-site whenever possible. Under some circumstances a job may take more time than we have available. At your discretion, I can take the bike back to my home shop for completion or be paid for time spent and return another day.

Prices

The standard prices listed are subject to change without notice. Individual part installation prices can be quoted upon request but are also subject to change without notice. Prices do NOT include parts.
